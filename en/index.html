Welcome to Densoka

Who are we?

We are a small Finnish Discord community server. Densoka started at 19.5.2022 and it has became an active Discord server. We offer a friendly community to belong and spent time.

How to give feedback?

You can give us feedback at Google Forms via this link. We do appreciate all your feedback and try to make the community even greater with it.
